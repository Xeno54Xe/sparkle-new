## 1. Executive Summary

Bajaj Finance reported a robust Q2 FY26 performance with a 24% year-over-year growth in Assets Under Management (AUM), reaching INR 462,261 crores. The company added 4.13 million new customers, contributing to a total customer base of 110.64 million. Despite elevated credit costs, the company maintained steady Return on Assets (ROA) and Return on Equity (ROE). However, the AUM growth guidance was revised downwards to 22-23% from the previous 24-25% due to risk management actions in the MSME segment.

Management emphasized strategic themes such as risk management, operational efficiency, and technological transformation, particularly through AI. The company is phasing out its captive 2-wheeler and 3-wheeler finance business to improve asset quality and is focusing on new business lines like gold loans and new car loans. The management reiterated its commitment to long-term growth while managing short-term risks prudently.

The overall tone of the call was cautiously bullish.
